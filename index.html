Game of Flames

Instructions:

  • Use the up, down, left and right arrow keys to block.
  • Each wall you build will disappear after 0.5s.
  • If you get hit, you lose.
  • Level One: Block 30 fireballs to advance.
  • Level Two: Block 40 fireballs to advance.
  • Level Three: Block 50 fireballs to win the game.
  • The speed will increase after each level.
